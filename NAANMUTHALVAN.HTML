Pasta Carbonara

A classic Italian dish with eggs, cheese, and pancetta.

View Full Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 200g fettuccine
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g grated Parmesan
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Cook pasta.
  2. Fry pancetta.
  3. Mix eggs and cheese.
  4. Combine.

Chocolate Muffins

Rich and indulgent chocolate baked goods.

View Full Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup cocoa
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Bake at 350°F for 20 mins.
  2. Mix batter.
  3. Fold in chips.
  4. Fill muffin cups.
